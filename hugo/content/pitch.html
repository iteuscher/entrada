Our Services

✻

We invest in people, not products. We won't just write checks, or look at dozens of startups each quarter, rather we work personally and intensely with no more than 10 companies each year helping them develop from late seed stage companies to their first round of professional financing. Here are a few of the things our team helps with:

Pitch and Pitch Deck Perfection

You often get one shot with an investor. We want to ensure you’re in the best position to capitalize on any opportunity that comes your way.

Talent acquisition

You might have nailed product market fit, but without the right team in place your business can only go so far. Let us leverage our network to help you build the perfect team.

Intellectual Property Protection

Why you continue to focus on building your business, we can help you protect the assets you invested so much time and energy creating.

Cap Table Clean Up

It won’t help you sell more product or find more customers, but it will help streamline the fundraising process by eliminating a friction point with many investors. Our experience in this area will simplify your fundraising efforts.

KPI Determination and Monitoring

Establishing key performance indicators upfront will help you and your team focus on what’s important and make it easier for investors to gain confidence in your business.

Intro to Appropriate VC Firms

When the time is right we can help you navigate the venture world and not just make introductions, but point you to the appropriate firms and partners based on your business and stage.
